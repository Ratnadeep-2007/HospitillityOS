# Product Name

**Daily Operations Hub (V1)**

---

# Product Statement

Daily Operations Hub is an AI-powered operational workspace that transforms daily hotel activities into structured, trackable, and coordinated workflows.

Instead of relying on WhatsApp messages, phone calls, Excel sheets, or human memory, every operational task is automatically created, assigned, tracked, and completed through one unified platform.

The goal of Version 1 is simple:

> **Make sure nothing important happening inside the hotel is forgotten, delayed, or unmanaged.**

---

# Build Scope

Version 1 focuses only on **daily operational execution**.

It does **not** attempt to replace the hotel's existing PMS, POS, accounting system, or booking engine.

Instead, Version 1 becomes the hotel's operational layer.

The MVP supports operational activities generated by:

- Guest Requests
- Hotel Bookings
- Daily SOPs
- Manager Instructions
- Basic Inventory Alerts
- Scheduled Maintenance

Every activity becomes a structured operational task.

---

# Hero Workflow

The core workflow of Version 1 is:

```

Operational Event

↓

AI Understands Event

↓

Creates Operational Task

↓

Assigns Responsible Department

↓

Tracks Progress

↓

Escalates Delays

↓

Manager Visibility

↓

Completion Recorded

```

Every feature in Version 1 supports this workflow.

# Core Features Included

---

## 1. Unified Operational Inbox

All operational events arrive in one place.

Sources include:

- Guest requests
- Front desk
- Manager-created tasks
- SOP triggers
- Inventory alerts
- Maintenance schedules

---

## 2. AI Request Understanding

AI converts natural language into structured operational tasks.

Example

Guest says

"I need two extra pillows and airport pickup tomorrow."

AI extracts

- Pillows
- Airport Pickup
- Date
- Priority

Then creates tasks automatically.

---

## 3. Smart Task Engine

Automatically creates:

- Task
- Priority
- Department
- Due time
- Assignee
- Dependencies

Tasks can also be created manually.

---

## 4. Automatic Task Routing

Tasks are assigned automatically to:

- Reception
- Housekeeping
- Kitchen
- Maintenance
- Procurement

Managers may manually override assignments.

---

## 5. Department Dashboards

Each department sees only its own work.

Examples

Housekeeping

- Rooms to clean
- Delayed rooms
- VIP rooms

Maintenance

- Repairs
- Scheduled maintenance

Kitchen

- Food requests
- Special dietary requests

---

## 6. Manager Dashboard

Managers can view:

- Active Tasks
- Delayed Tasks
- Department Workload
- Escalations
- Daily Operations
- Staff Status

---

## 7. SOP Engine

The system automatically creates recurring tasks.

Examples

Morning Opening

↓

Reception Checklist

↓

Restaurant Opening

↓

Pool Inspection

↓

Security Patrol

No manual reminders are required.

---

## 8. Basic Inventory Alerts

Departments can define minimum stock levels.

Example

Milk

Current

5 litres

Minimum

10 litres

↓

System creates inventory alert.

Version 1 only creates alerts.

Automatic purchasing is not included.

---

## 9. Scheduled Maintenance

Managers define maintenance schedules.

Examples

- Generator
- AC
- Swimming Pool
- Fire Safety
- Elevators

The system automatically generates maintenance tasks.

---

## 10. Notifications

Notify:

- Assigned Staff
- Supervisors
- Managers

Channels

- In-app
- Push Notifications
- Email (optional)

---

## 11. Activity Timeline

Every task records:

- Created
- Assigned
- Updated
- Completed
- Escalated

Complete audit history is maintained.

# Features Explicitly Excluded

The following features are intentionally excluded from Version 1.

---

## Reservation Management

No room booking engine.

No room inventory management.

No OTA synchronization.

---

## Billing

No invoicing.

No payments.

No accounting.

---

## Revenue Intelligence

No dynamic pricing.

No revenue forecasting.

No demand prediction.

---

## Vendor Management

Only inventory alerts.

No purchase orders.

No vendor portal.

No invoice management.

---

## Predictive AI

No failure prediction.

No workload prediction.

No occupancy prediction.

---

## Advanced Analytics

Only operational dashboards.

No advanced BI.

---

## Multi-Property Management

Version 1 supports one property.

Chain management comes later.

---

## AI Decision Making

AI may recommend.

AI cannot automatically execute critical business decisions.

Humans remain in control.

# Success Metrics

Version 1 will be considered successful if it achieves measurable operational improvements.

## Operational Metrics

- ≥95% of operational tasks are digitally tracked.
- ≥90% of tasks have assigned owners.
- ≥85% task completion within SLA.
- Reduction in missed operational requests.
- Reduction in manual follow-ups.

---

## Staff Metrics

- Daily active usage by operational staff.
- Average task completion time.
- Supervisor response time.
- Manager visibility across departments.

---

## Guest Experience Metrics

- Faster response to guest requests.
- Reduction in unresolved complaints.
- Improved service consistency.

---

## Business Metrics

- Reduced operational delays.
- Increased staff productivity.
- Reduced dependence on WhatsApp and paper tracking.
- Higher operational transparency.

---

# Out of Scope

Version 1 is **not** intended to become a full Property Management System.

Version 1 does not replace existing software.

Its purpose is to become the operational coordination layer that sits above existing systems and enables hotels to execute daily work consistently.

---

# Definition of Success

Version 1 succeeds when hotel staff no longer ask:

- "Who was supposed to do this?"
- "Did anyone inform housekeeping?"
- "Was this completed?"
- "Who is responsible?"
- "What still needs to be done today?"

Instead, every operational activity is visible, assigned, tracked, and completed through a single operational platform.

Version 1 is complete when the platform becomes the default operational workspace used by every department every day.
