# DEPARTMENTS.md

> **Document Purpose**
>
> This document defines how every department within a hospitality property interacts with the AI Hospitality Operations Platform.
>
> It explains each department's responsibilities, daily workflows, permissions, dashboards, AI assistance, and interactions with other departments.
>
> The goal is to ensure every department operates through one unified operational platform while maintaining clear ownership and accountability.

---

# Department Interaction Model

Every department follows the same operational lifecycle.

```
Receive Work

↓

View Assigned Tasks

↓

Execute Work

↓

Update Status

↓

Request Assistance (if needed)

↓

Complete Task

↓

Platform Updates Other Departments

↓

Operational History Stored
```

---

# 1. Front Desk (Reception)

## Primary Responsibility

The Front Desk is the operational gateway between guests and the hotel.

Most guest interactions begin here.

---

## Daily Responsibilities

- Guest Check-in
- Guest Check-out
- Guest Requests
- Room Assignment
- Guest Communication
- Issue Reporting
- Manual Task Creation
- Coordination with Departments

---

## Platform Capabilities

The platform enables the Front Desk to:

- Create guest requests
- View guest history
- Create operational tasks
- Track task progress
- View room readiness
- Escalate unresolved requests
- Receive department updates

---

## Dashboard

Displays:

- Current Check-ins
- Current Check-outs
- Guest Requests
- Pending Tasks
- VIP Arrivals
- Delayed Requests
- Room Status

---

## AI Assistance

AI can:

- Understand guest requests
- Suggest task priority
- Recommend departments
- Summarize guest conversations
- Detect urgent requests

---

## Interacts With

- Housekeeping
- Maintenance
- Kitchen
- Restaurant
- Spa
- Security
- Manager

# Department Collaboration

The platform is designed so departments no longer operate independently.

Instead of communicating through:

- Phone calls
- WhatsApp
- Paper notes
- Verbal instructions

Departments collaborate through structured operational workflows.

Every task has:

- One owner
- One status
- One deadline
- Complete visibility
- Full audit history

The platform ensures every department shares the same operational picture, reducing communication gaps and improving service consistency across the property.
