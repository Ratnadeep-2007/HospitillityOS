# ROADMAP.md

> **Document Purpose**
>
> This document defines the long-term evolution of the AI Hospitality Operations Platform.
>
> It explains how the platform grows from an operational task management system into an intelligent, AI-driven hospitality operating system.
>
> Each version builds on the previous one while maintaining a stable architecture and user experience.

---

# Product Evolution

The platform evolves through four stages.

```
Operational Coordination

        ↓

Operational Expansion

        ↓

Operational Intelligence

        ↓

Autonomous Hospitality Operations
```

Each stage introduces new capabilities while preserving the same operational foundation.

---

# Long-Term Vision — Autonomous Hospitality Operations

## Vision

Create the world's first AI-powered operational brain for hospitality.

The platform evolves from assisting staff to coordinating operations autonomously while keeping humans in control of strategic and guest-facing decisions.

---

## Future Capabilities

### Autonomous Coordination

The platform automatically:

- Assigns work
- Balances workloads
- Adjusts priorities
- Resolves operational conflicts

---

### Predictive Operations

The platform predicts:

- Guest demand
- Occupancy
- Maintenance failures
- Inventory shortages
- Staffing requirements
- Revenue opportunities

---

### Intelligent Resource Optimization

Optimize:

- Staff Allocation
- Inventory Usage
- Energy Consumption
- Maintenance Scheduling
- Vendor Selection

---

### Multi-Property Intelligence

Organizations operating multiple hotels receive:

- Portfolio-wide dashboards
- Cross-property benchmarking
- Shared operational knowledge
- AI recommendations across properties

---

### Continuous Learning

The platform continuously learns from:

- Operational history
- Staff actions
- Guest behavior
- Seasonal demand
- Property performance

Every recommendation becomes more accurate over time.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every future version follows these principles:

- Build on the same Event Engine.
- Build on the same Workflow Engine.
- Keep humans in control of critical decisions.
- Integrate before replacing existing systems.
- AI should assist before it automates.
- Every feature must improve operational efficiency.
- Every module must integrate seamlessly with the rest of the platform.

# Final Product Evolution

```
V1

Coordinate Work

↓

V2

Connect Operations

↓

V3

Optimize Operations

↓

Future

Autonomous Hospitality Operations
```

The roadmap reflects a deliberate progression:

- **First**, ensure work is visible and coordinated.
- **Then**, connect the entire operational ecosystem.
- **Next**, use accumulated operational data to make better decisions.
- **Finally**, enable AI to proactively optimize hospitality operations while maintaining human oversight for critical business decisions.
